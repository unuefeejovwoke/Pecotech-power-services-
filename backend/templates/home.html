Discover FAQ from our support

How does solar panel installation work?

Solar panel installation involves mounting the panels on suitable structures, connecting them to the electrical system, and ensuring proper alignment for maximum sunlight exposure.

What are the benefits of switching to solar energy?

Switching to solar energy offers several benefits, including reduced electricity bills, lower carbon footprint, potential financial incentives, and increased energy independence.

How long do solar panels typically last?

Solar panels generally have a lifespan of 25 to 30 years or more, with most manufacturers providing warranties ranging from 20 to 25 years.

Can solar panels work during cloudy or rainy days?

Yes, solar panels can still generate electricity on cloudy or rainy days, although their efficiency may be reduced compared to sunny days.

What maintenance is required for solar panels?

Solar panels generally require minimal maintenance. Periodic cleaning, inspecting for debris or damage, and ensuring proper electrical connections are recommended

What factors should I consider when designing a custom solar system?

Factors to consider include your energy consumption, available roof or ground space, orientation and tilt of panels, shading, and local regulations.
